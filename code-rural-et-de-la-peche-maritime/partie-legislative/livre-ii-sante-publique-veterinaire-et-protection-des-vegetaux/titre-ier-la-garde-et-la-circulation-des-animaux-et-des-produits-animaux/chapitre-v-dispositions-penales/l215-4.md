# Article L215-4

L211-14 (V)'>articles L. 211-14 </a>et <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000006071367&idArticle=LEGIARTI000006583059&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code rural - art. L211-16 (V)'>L. 211-16</a>.
