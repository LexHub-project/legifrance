# Article D151-27

D151-25 (V)'>articles D. 151-25 </a>et <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000006071367&idArticle=LEGIARTI000006587563&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code rural - art.
