# Article L315-6

L315-20 (VD)'>L. 315-20 </a>et <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000006069565&idArticle=LEGIARTI000032222449&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code de la consommation - art. L315-21 (V)'>L. 315-21</a>.</p>
