# Article L128-3

L127-1 (VT)'>articles    L. 127-1, L. 127-2 </a>, <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000006074075&idArticle=LEGIARTI000031063562&dateTexte=&categorieLien=id' title='Code de l'urbanisme - art.
