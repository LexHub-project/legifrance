# Article L223-7

L223-8 (V)'>223-8 </a>à L. <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000045476241&idArticle=LEGIARTI000045478451&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code pénitentiaire - art. L223-15 (V)'>223-15</a>.</p>
