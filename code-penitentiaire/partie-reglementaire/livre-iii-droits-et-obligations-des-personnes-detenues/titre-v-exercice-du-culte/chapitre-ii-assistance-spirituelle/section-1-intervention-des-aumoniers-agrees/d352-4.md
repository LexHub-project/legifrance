# Article D352-4

L112-4 (V)'>L. 112-4 </a>et <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000045476241&idArticle=LEGIARTI000045487338&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code pénitentiaire - art. R112-22 (V)'>R. 112-22</a>.</p>
