R622-46 (V)'>R. 622-46</a>, <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000006074236&idArticle=LEGIARTI000024242309&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code du patrimoine - art. R622-50 (V)'>R. 622-50</a>, <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000006074236&idArticle=LEGIARTI000024242311&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code du patrimoine - art.
