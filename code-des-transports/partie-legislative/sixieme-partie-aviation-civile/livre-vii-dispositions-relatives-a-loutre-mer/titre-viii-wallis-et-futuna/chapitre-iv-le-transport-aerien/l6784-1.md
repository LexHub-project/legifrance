L6432-3 (V)'>L. 6432-3</a>. </p><p>Les articles <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000023086525&idArticle=LEGIARTI000045848864&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code des transports - art.
