# Article L6122-15

L6122-16 (V)'>articles L. 6122-16 </a>et <a href='/affichCodeArticle.do?cidTexte=LEGITEXT000023086525&idArticle=LEGIARTI000023075399&dateTexte=&categorieLien=cid' title='Code des transports - art. L6122-19 (V)'>L. 6122-19</a>.</p>
